Welcome to Second Chance Books
Book Exchange

We're glad you're here we hope you enjoy looking through our library. Our site makes it easy to exchange books. If you share a book you're done reading receive one book credit. Redeem one book credit for one bookshelf item.

Our mission is to give books the love and appreciation they deserve by providing them a second chance to create inspiration.
